Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I purchase?

After successful payment, you'll receive an email with instructions for downloading and installing the app.

Can I use this on multiple computers?

The single-user license allows installation on up to 3 devices for personal use. For team licenses, please contact us.

Do I need an OpenAI API key?

Yes, you'll need an OpenAI API key for AI transcription features. Creating an account is free, and you only pay for what you use (~$0.05 per document). Full setup instructions are included.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All documents are stored locally on your computer. Your OpenAI API key stays on your device, and no telemetry or tracking is performed. You control your data completely.
