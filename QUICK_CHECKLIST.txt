  → Go to: dashboard.stripe.com/payment-links
  → Click "New payment link"
  → Name: Faith & Fitness Challenge Guide
  → Price: $5.00
  → Enable "Collect email"
  → COPY THE LINK!

□ Step 3: Update Sales Page (5 min)
  → Open downloads-simple.html
